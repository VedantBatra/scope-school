PROMINENT CLUBS VIT

CUBE CLUB VIT

The purpose of C.U.B.E VIT is to serve as a space where individuals can gather to learn about, practice, compete, and take joy in solving various twisty puzzles, such as the 3x3x3 Rubik’s cube. We aim to be a welcoming environment for individuals of any skill level, be it speed cubing professional or first-time solver, and to be an educational resource for anyone interested in puzzle solving, whether they want to be a regular member or not.

YOUTH RED CROSS VIT

“Youth Red Cross” is a student club of VIT, Vellore. It is an official body, of students, that encourages social welfare and selfless service to life.It is an official body of students that encourages social welfare by organizing Blood Donation camps, First Aid Training camps, Anti-smoking Campaigns, Visits and help to Orphanages, etc
